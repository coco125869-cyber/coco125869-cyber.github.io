SDGs Exhibition Audience Quiz

Answer 5 questions to get your recommended pathway (Professionals/Hobbyists vs Explorers).
1) Which reading style fits you better?
2) Have you taken any courses or participated in any events related to UNESCO’s Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) at school or in other institutions?
3) What is your main purpose for visiting this exhibition?
4) What does the term "SDG" first bring to your mind?
5) What is your expected viewing time for the SDGs-themed sections?
Scoring: sum option values. Threshold: total ≥ 5 → Professionals/Hobbyists; total < 5 → Explorers.
